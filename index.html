About your tutor

I know what it takes to truly learn a language

I'm a Croatian tutor based in Rijeka, and I speak six languages. That's not a party trick — it means I understand learning a language from the inside out: the frustration of grammar rules that don't make sense, the joy of the first real conversation, the patience it takes to get there.

I offer warm, structured, and flexible online lessons tailored to you — whether you're a complete beginner, preparing for an exam, moving to a new country, or raising a bilingual child.

Every lesson is 1-on-1. No big groups, no cookie-cutter syllabi. Just us, your goals, and a plan that works.
